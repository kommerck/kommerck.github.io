Leon Freitag

I am a researcher at the University of Vienna at the Institute of Theoretical Chemistry in the group of Prof. Leticia González. In my research I develop quantum chemical methods and employ them to accurately predict excited states of transition metal complexes. This allows us to design better artificial photosynthesis systems or light-activated anticancer drugs.

I have a strong background in scientific computing and am a co-author of two (not very well-known) quantum chemistry codes OpenMolcas and QCMaquis. I’m also interested in machine learning.
